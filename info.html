In case you encounter any issues here is a list with the most common issues and their solutions:

  • If you forgot your password or it doesn't work, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" button on the login page or on the "Change password" button on the profile page.
  • If you can't login, make sure you have an account and that you are using the correct credentials.
  • If you opened a page you didn't want to, you can go back by clicking the back button/home button or click on the title at the top of the page.
  • If you have any other issues, you can contact the creator of the website on Discord. Username: xevvon.
